# Day 4 – 3PL & Logistics Management

## 1. Levels of Logistics Management

### 1PL – First Party Logistics
- Seller delivers goods using own resources
- Direct delivery

### 2PL – Second Party Logistics
- Transportation companies
- Courier or shipper moves goods

### 3PL – Third Party Logistics
- Warehousing
- Transportation
- Order fulfillment
- Inventory management

### 4PL – Fourth Party Logistics
- Supply chain consulting
- Manages multiple 3PLs
- End-to-end coordination

### 5PL – Fifth Party Logistics
- Full network optimization
- Digital, multi-client logistics ecosystems

---

## 2. 3PL in 6 Steps

1. Seller ships finished products to 3PL warehouse
2. 3PL receives and logs inventory
3. Orders flow from seller platforms to 3PL
4. Orders are picked and packed
5. Orders are shipped
6. Customer receives delivery

---

## 3. Role of 3PL in Supply Chain

3PL plays a vital role in:
- Planning
- Order Management
- Inventory Management
- Storage
- Transportation
- Reverse Logistics
- Resource optimization (space, manpower, IT systems, vehicles)

---

## 4. Benefits of 3PL Services

- Focus on core business
- No warehouse ownership required
- Faster shipping
- Cost savings and efficiency gains
- Scalability on demand
- Access to skilled manpower and advanced technology
- Risk mitigation during supply chain disruptions
- Improved customer satisfaction

---

## 5. Large 3PL Companies (Examples)

- UPS
- FedEx
- DHL
- Maersk Logistics
- XPO Logistics
- CEVA Logistics
- GEODIS
- GATI (India)

---

## 6. Key Takeaways
- 3PL is not just transportation; it is **execution ownership**
- Companies outsource logistics to gain **flexibility, speed, and expertise**
- Modern supply chains cannot scale without 3PL support
